About Me

Meet Victoria

I have been an esthetician (skin care therapist) since graduating from St. Paul College in 2006. I have a passion for making people good about the way they look and love to help teach people about what taking care of their skin really takes. Most people don’t know just how effective good skin care can be for your skin; or how ineffective bad skin care can be. It’s all about the ingredients in the products and the science of how the skin absorbs and uses those ingredients.

I enjoy being a skin therapist at Sudz Salon in uptown Minneapolis. I work with two of the best skin care lines in the field; Cosmedix and Glymed. I love learning about the ingredients and how and why they work for your skin.
